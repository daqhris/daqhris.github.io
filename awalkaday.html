About | A WALK≡A DAY

Hi, I'm very glad that you have discovered A WALK≡A DAY.

Frequently Asked Questions:


WHAT

Born out of simple boredom and burst of creativity, A WALK≡A DAY is a black and white visual arts project.

Since its inception, every long walk ends with a collection of black & white photos snapped on-the-go by using a smartphone camera.


WHEN

The first image was published on Instagram (@walk.day) on July 19, 2017.

At least one picture is (or should be) posted on a daily basis.

This art project is everlasting.


WHY

One motivational quote inspired the creation of the project : "The sun is up and running, everyday, for you."

In a global trend of fast-paced urbanization, generations of humans are growing without a sense of curiosity about their surroundings and adventurous drive in unfamiliar landscapes.

Instagram, with millions of daily active users, was selected as the most compelling online platform to host such a street photography gallery that would target millenials and inspire them to creatively lead a healthy lifestyle.


HOW

Images are shared on Instagram in a black and white format with a wide white frame.

All pictures are taken from an Android smartphone and later, enhanced by using photo-editing applications.

The hashtag #awalkaday is attached to every post and can be clicked on to discover similar posts shared by the Instagram community.


WHERE

Photos are taken at any time of the day from the streets and hidden alleys of Western Europe.

The photographer is currently based in Europe.


WHO

The person behind the #awalkaday art project is Chris-Armel (@iamdaqhris).

He was born in Burundi and has previously lived in East Asia and North Africa.
