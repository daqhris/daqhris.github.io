Chris A. Iradukunda was born in November 1992 in Bujumbura, BURUNDI.
He attended primary school at Ecole Primaire Stella Matutina and high school at Lycée du Saint-Esprit de Bujumbura with an emphasis on Maths and Physics.
At 20 years old, he began studying Chinese Language and taking college preparatory lessons at Tianjin University (China).
In 2013, he enrolled in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Science and Technology, Beijing as a scholarship-sponsored undergrad.
As of 2017, he was pursuing his passion in the CS field at University of Namur, Belgium.

Besides being a studious and hard-working individual, Chris-Armel often strives to land a useful hand to his community and brings to fruition diverse side projects.
During primary school, he was involved in the Scout movement as part of the Charles de Foucauld Unit, situated across a town river (Muha river) at the edge of his home district (Kinanira II area).
Then, at the Jesuits' prestigious high school, he participated in different on-campus student clubs which led to him leading the 'Akanuma Journal' student club as editor-in-chief and geeking late at night in the computer lab.
After graduation(2011), he received the Best Devotion to School Award and later, was granted a full scholarship to a Chinese university.

Over a year and half period, he was learning from scratch the Chinese language and touring the North-Eastern region of the Middle Kingdom during school vacation to soak up its vast culture and history.
Starting from 2013, he attended university in Beijing and embarked on a 4-years major in Computer Science.
Out of the classroom, he would usually hang around the Zhongguancun 'tech hive' area and take part into hackathons or discussions delving into topics of FinTech and AI.
Concurrently, he kept his traveler hat on and then, by sheer luck, landed gigs as a movie 'extra' actor and as a foreign actors recruiter for major Chinese blockbusters(2015-2016).

He speaks fluently his mother-tongue Kirundi, French, English and Chinese. He can also maintain light conversations in Swahili, Arabic and Dutch.
Chris is very much into reading books, photography(black-and-white formats), outdoor activities(biking, swimming), coding in Javascript/Python and current research in Artificial Intelligence.
Sometimes, he may get opinionated on political issues. Oups!

Recently, you might have met him either in Egypt or Belgium.
Anyway, whoever you are, he is open to having a cup of tea/coffee, collaborate, make on-the-spot funny comedy sketchs or improvise dance moves with you.
