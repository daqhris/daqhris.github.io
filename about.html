about me


Chris A. IRADUKUNDA was born in 1992 in Bujumbura, Burundi.
He attended primary school at École Primaire Stella Matutina and secondary school at Lycée du Saint-Esprit de Bujumbura with an emphasis on Maths and Physics.
In 2012, he enrolled to study Computer Science and Engineering at University of Science and Technology, Beijing (China).
As of 2017, he's pursuing a degree in the same field at University of Namur (Belgium) and volunteers at a bicycle repair shop.

During primary school, he was involved in the Scout Movement as part of the Charles de Foucauld unit.
Later on, at a Jesuit's high school, he participated in various on-campus student clubs and led the Akanuma Journal as editor-in-chief.
After graduation, he received the Best Devotion to School Award and was granted a full-ride scholarship at a Chinese university.

Over a five-year period, while studying in Beijing, he worked in the film industry as a background actor and as a foreign talent manager for major Chinese blockbusters (China Salesman, Wolf Warrior 2, My War,...).

He fluently speaks Kirundi, French, English and Mandarin Chinese.
Moreover, he can hold conversations in Swahili, Arabic and Dutch languages.
