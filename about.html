about me


Chris A. IRADUKUNDA was born in 1992 in Bujumbura, Burundi.
He attended primary school at École Primaire Stella Matutina and secondary school at Lycée du Saint-Esprit de Bujumbura with an emphasis on Maths and Physics.
Later, he enrolled in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Science and Technology, Beijing (China).
From 2017, he is pursuing his passion in the Computer Science field at University of Namur (Belgium) and volunteering at a bicycle repair shop.


Besides being a studious and hard-working individual, Mr. IRADUKUNDA often strives to lend a hand to his local community and brings to fruition diverse side projects.
During primary school, he was involved in the Scout Movement as part of the Charles de Foucauld unit.
At the Jesuit's prestigious high school, he participated in various on-campus student clubs which ultimately resulted in him leading the Akanuma Journal (high school newspaper) as editor-in-chief and coding late at night in the computer lab.
Shortly after graduation in 2011, he received the Best Devotion to School Award and later, was granted a full-ride scholarship to a Chinese university.


Over a five-year period, while he was living in Beijing, he toured the northeastern and nortwestern region of China and gladly experienced its amazing culture and history.
Concurrently, he landed part-time jobs as a background actor and as a foreign talent manager for major Chinese blockbusters (China Salesman, Wolf Warrior 2, My War,...).


He fluently speaks Kirundi, French, English and Mandarin Chinese.
He can also make light conversations in Swahili, Arabic and Dutch.
Chris is fond of reading books, photography (black-and-white formats) and outdoor activities (biking, swimming).


More often than not, he will aggree to share a cup of tea/coffee, to discuss or to effectively contribute to challenging projects.
