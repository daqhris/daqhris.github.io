Hello, I'm very glad that you have discovered A WALK≡A DAY!

INTRO
Born out of simple boredom and burst of creativity, A WALK≡A DAY is a black and white visual arts project.
Since then, every long walk ends with a collection of B&W photos snapped on-the-go by using a smartphone camera.

WHEN ?
The first image was published on Instagram (@all.day.walking) on July 19th, 2017.
At least one picture is (or should be) posted on a daily basis.
This art project's lifespan is 5 years.

WHY ?
A single motivating quote essentially inspired the creation of the project : "The Sun is up and running, everyday, for you."
In a global trend of fast-paced urbanization, generations of kids are growing without a sense of curiosity about their surroundings and adventurous drive in unfamiliar landscapes.
Instagram, with 400 million daily active users, was selected as the most compelling online platform to host such a street photography gallery that would target millenials and inspire them to creatively lead a healthy lifestyle.

HOW ?
Images are shared on Instagram in a black and white format with a wide white frame.
All pictures are taken from an Android phone and later, edited in Snapseed.
The hashtag #awalkaday is attached to every post and can be clicked on to discover similar posts shared by the Instagram community.

WHERE ?
Photos are taken at any time of the day from the streets and hidden alleys of Western Europe.
I'm currently based in Belgium.

WHO ?
The person behind the #awalkaday art project is Chris-Armel (@daqhris).
He was born in Burundi and has previously traveled to Eastern Asia, Middle East and North Africa.
